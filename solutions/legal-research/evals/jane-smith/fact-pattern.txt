Jane Smith ("Defendant") runs a blog (the "Blog") that does not have any advertisers. Global Media ("Plaintiff") owns a magazine. The magazine published an article (the "Magazine Article"). Smith quoted the heart of the Magazine Article in a post on the Blog (the "Blog Post"). The Blog Post did not include much content beyond the quoted portion of the Magazine Article. After quoting the Magazine Article, Smith criticized the article. The criticism was short relative to the part of the Magazine Article that she quoted. Smith's criticism was ruthless. Smith's criticism may have harmed the credibility of the Magazine. Global Media maintains that the Blog has cost the Magazine business. Global Media maintains that Smith benefited by seeing increased voluntary contributions to her Blog.
